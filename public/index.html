Why do we reward bnb

We believe these Tokenomincs should create a somewhat stable token that can be used in the DeFi space for applications of all kinds. Our holders will gain a fair share of the fees for providing price stability over time.

FAQ

How can I passively earn the 8% BNB reward?

You just need to hold FASTMOON Tokens to benefit from the daily reward. Beside the BNB reward there is a 2% reflection of FASTMOON Tokens on top which will be distributed to our holders automatically. There is no guarantee as the BNB reward depends on the transaction volume of the token itself. So if the transaction volume gets less, there will be less tokens to convert to BNB resulting in a lower pool

How much BNB can I withdraw?

You can claim BNB according to your share of the circulating supply. For example, if you hold 1% of all circulating FASTMOON Tokens, you get 1% BNB from the pool. Withdrawing more than 1 BNB will result in a 25% tax going to a tax wallet.

How can I claim my BNB reward?

Connect your MetaMask or Trustwallet with our app on our website. The data will then be synchronized and with the click on the button “Withdraw BNB Reward” your BNB reward will be transferred to your wallet.

When can I get my BNB?

Once you hold FASTMOON Tokens you can access the pool described above every 24 hours and withdraw your share. Keep in mind, if you add up more FASTMOON tokens within the waiting cycle, the duration increases by the percentage of FASTMOON tokens you added. The maximum cycle duration is 24 hours tho.

What is the BNB pool?

On each transaction there will be 16% tax of which 8% goes into the BNB pool. The pool is basically the contract itself where BNB get stored until they get claimed.

What happens if I forget to withdraw my BNB?

The amount of FASTMOON/BNB that is in the pool reduces the longer you wait. Therefore, it is recommended to withdraw the reward daily and as early as possible because other holders will claim and lower the amount of available BNB resulting in a lower share.

Does my BNB stack if I don't withdraw it?

No. To clarify: The BNB you can withdraw every 24 hours will always be allocated to your share at the current time and will not be set aside. We therefore recommend that you harvest your reward as early as possible.

How does the amount of BNB rewards gets calculated?

Several factors determine the amount of your reward:

1. The amount of BNB in the pool is distributed according to the shares of your FASTMOON tokens.

2. The BNB Pool decreases on every withdrawal and increases on each transaction, therefore you might see if fluctuating.

3. We would like to point out that FASTMOON is a holder token. Quickly skimming profits by selling high and buying low within a short period of time will result in the next withdrawal interval being postponed into the future to avoid people adding FASTMOON tokens and therefore rising their share right before their withdrawal.

Is the BNB reward guaranteed?

The BNB reward solely depends on the transaction volume of FASTMOON. The Token collects 8% of the tokens of each transaction as a fee and converts them into BNB adding them into the reward pool. Additionally the contract takes 6% and adds them to the LP pool and 2% which get redistributed to all holders including the burn wallet.
If there is no transaction volume, the BNB reward pool stays empty.
